SE Pennsylvania Analyses (made 2022)

A series of maps of Southeast Pennsylvania made for an introductory GIS course, including analyses of: forest cover and elevation in Bucks County, land for a new high school in New Abington, and the impacts of redlining in Philadelphia.

Arlington, VA Bicycle Lanes (made 2023)

Maps made for a 2023 analysis of bicycle lane access in Arlington County, Virginia, differentiated by type of lane. The full analysis and full-size copies of each map, as well as additional supplemental maps, can be found on the "Writing" page as Bicycle Route Access in Arlington County, Virginia.

Fairfax, VA Greyfield Candidates (made 2024)

The final step of my greyfields project, showing the results of a resource allocation operation that identified potential greyfield parcels (parcels of high impermeable surface area that were apparently not in use based on utility shutoff data) that were the best candidates for redevelopment based on proximity to disadvantaged areas of the county.

Arlington, VA Parks and Facilities Reservations (made 2024)

This interactive ArcGIS Online dashboard uses the Arlington County OpenData Portal API to update daily with data on parks and facilities reservations, allowing viewers to identify popular facilities, reservation times, and uses (full size).
